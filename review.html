I am from one of the old IIT. The ragging done here is known as Orientation Programme (OP). It happens when students just enter into their second year. The OP starts with some basic things which you need to learn by heart. One of these is ‘Introduction’ which is called ‘Intro’ in short. And the special thing about this ‘remembering by heart’ process is that you need to remember it 100% as exactly as it is. So, a sample Intro goes like this: My name is Rajesh Sharma. I am a second year undergraduate student of the department of Mechanical Engineering enrolled in its Dual degree course. I am from Allahabad. My hobbies are badminton, dramatics. So, seniors will make you stand in front of all and speak your Intro. If you miss even a single letter, you will have to restart from beginning and repeat. For example, if you said “of department of” instead of “of the department of” then you will have to restart and repeat. If you say “badminton and dramatics” instead of “badminton, dramatics”, you will have to restart and repeat. If you pause somewhere in between for more than 2 seconds, you will have to restart and repeat. This process goes on until you perfectly deliver your Intro with fluency. The Intro session was going on. Some seniors and all second years were there. Seniors were calling second years randomly and telling them to give their Intro. One guy wasn’t able to pass this Intro test. He was doing good but he was just missing ‘the’, ‘a’, pausing while speaking and other little things. As a result, he was retrying from start again and again. After 15 minutes when he had tried delivering Intro around 40 times, one senior came to him. The conversation, in front of entire crowd, went this way: Senior: You can’t speak even a few lines properly. What will you do in your life? You won’t be able to do anything in your life. He: (heads down) (silent). Senior: (with intention to shame him more) Seriously, what will you do in your life! I guarantee that you won’t do anything in your life if you can’t give a simple Intro. He: (head still down) (silent). Senior: (with intention to shame him even more) So, you think that you are very good at academics. Hence, you don’t require such soft skills. One who can’t give an Intro, he can’t be good even at his studies. I bet you aren’t good at your studies even. What’s your CGPA (grade)? He: (head up) 9.81 There was stunned silence in the entire hall and everyone was laughing like hell inside as you can’t laugh openly during OP. Well, that senior also went silent and was embarrassed like hell. P.S.- CGPA of 9.81 (on a scale of 10) is considered awesome. Going anonymous because that senior is my good friend and I don’t want to screw that bond.
